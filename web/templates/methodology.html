Points Prediction Methodology

Overview

Our prediction model uses a sophisticated algorithm that combines multiple factors to estimate player performance. The model takes into account recent form, fixture difficulty, historical performance, and position-specific metrics to provide accurate predictions.

Key Factors

Recent Form (40%)

  • • Last 5 games performance
  • • Minutes played
  • • Goals and assists
  • • Clean sheets for defenders/goalkeepers

Fixture Difficulty (25%)

  • • Opponent strength
  • • Home/away factor
  • • Historical head-to-head performance

Historical Data (20%)

  • • Season performance
  • • Points per game
  • • Consistency rating

Team Context (15%)

  • • Team's attacking/defensive strength
  • • Team's recent form
  • • Player's role in the team

Position-Specific Considerations

Goalkeepers

Clean sheet probability, save potential, and team defensive strength are the primary factors.

Defenders

Combines clean sheet probability with attacking potential for full-backs and set-piece threats.

Midfielders

Balanced between attacking returns and clean sheet points, with additional focus on bonus point potential.

Forwards

Primary focus on goal-scoring probability and assist potential, considering team's attacking strength.

Model Accuracy

Our model is continuously updated and validated against actual performance data. Historical accuracy shows predictions within ±2 points for 70% of predictions. The model performs best for consistent starters and may have higher variance for rotation-risk players.
